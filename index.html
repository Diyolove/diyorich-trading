About Us

A Trusted Partner
Across Four Sectors

Diyorich Trading PLC was established in September 2018 by Mr. Dinbiyo Mohammed Seid and Ms. Fatuma Mohammed Ebrahim. We are a leading Ethiopian company engaged in importing a wide range of goods, equipment, machinery, and delivering comprehensive procurement solutions throughout Ethiopia.

We source products from Europe (UK, Germany, Italy, France, Switzerland), the United States, Japan, China, India, and the Middle East — bringing global markets to your doorstep at the best possible price.
